[Active] Research Intern @Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University

You will be involved with:

  • Cutting-edge compression related research
And you are expected to show:
  • Familiarity with deep/machine learning
  • Familiarity with image/video/model compression/density estimation
  • [is a plus] Familiarity with Computational Photography / Image Signal Processing (isp) pipeline

[Active] Research Intern @Sensetime Research

I am no longer advising interns in Sensetime Research. If you are interested in working with me, apply the intern position in Tsinghua. If you are interested in working in Sensetime Research, send me a resume and I will refer you to my colleagues in Sensetime.

You will be involved with:

  • Cutting-edge image & video coding research
And you are expected to show:
  • Familiarity with C/C++ & Python
  • Familiarity with one of the video coding standard (e.g. H.264/5/6, VP8/9, AV1, AVS, etc.)
  • Familiarity with one of the aspects of image or video coding research (e.g. entropy coding, rate control, quality metric, etc.)
  • [is a plus] Familiarity with image video processing or computer vision
  • [is a plus] Familiarity with machine learning or deep learning

Perspective Intern

Kindly email your target position, resume and availability to x.tongda@nyu.edu , I will reach back to you for interview if you are a good fit. If you are a undergraduate student in Tsinghua University, I suggest you check Student Research Training (SRT) projects and search for compression related projects by AIR.
